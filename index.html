SAMPE I-Beam

Manufactured with pre-preg carbon fiber. Manufacturing process included carbon fiber layup, lamination, fitting to mold, and using autoclave for curing. Extra material added to web-flange intersection and mid-section of the bottom side to increase ultimate strength. Additionally, fiber direction was determined by whether a particular section of the beam was in tension or compression.
